Welcome to CUNY Refiner

One quick setup makes everyday use much faster.

  1. 1

    Pin CUNY Refiner to your toolbar

    Open the browser extensions menu, find CUNY Refiner, and click the pin icon so it stays in your top menu bar.

  2. 2

    Click the CUNY Refiner icon

    Use the pinned icon to open settings and configure your default institution and term preferences.

Tip: Once your preferences are saved, CUNY Refiner will use them automatically in supported pages.

You can close this tab any time after setup.
